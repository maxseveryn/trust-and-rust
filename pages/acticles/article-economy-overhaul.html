News Update: Trust & Rust Economy Overhaul

Revamped Trading System:

In response to player feedback, Trust & Rust has implemented a comprehensive overhaul of the in-game trading system. Expect a more streamlined and secure process for exchanging resources, items, and blueprints within the community.

New Currency System:

One of the significant additions to the game is the introduction of Trust Coins, a brand-new in-game currency. Players can now earn Trust Coins through various in-game activities, providing them with a valuable resource to unlock exclusive items, skins, and other perks. This innovative currency system aims to enhance the in-game economy, offering a rewarding experience for players who invest time and effort into the game.

Marketplace Expansion:

The Trust&Rust Marketplace has expanded its offerings. Browse a wider array of items, from rare weapons to custom skins, available for purchase with Trust Coins. Engage in the bustling marketplace and elevate your Rust experience.
